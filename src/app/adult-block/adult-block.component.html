What Is AdultBlock?

It represents a straightforward mechanism that enables qualifying organizations to block the registration of any domain that falls under their trademark. This includes unlimited variants based on the holder's labels.

Instead of worrying about registering and managing many different adult related TLDs, it is possible to block trademarked domains under all four TLDs for up to ten years through just one action.

For example:

If YourCo.com qualified for this product, we could block YourCo.sex, YourCo.xxx, YourCo.adult, YourCo.porn, as well as all other variations.
